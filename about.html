About NutriSmart AI

NutriSmart AI is an enterprise computing solution designed to improve nutritional awareness through artificial intelligence and interactive media.

The system aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3: Good Health and Wellbeing.

The platform demonstrates enterprise computing concepts including data science, intelligent systems, user experience design, data visualisation, and cybersecurity awareness.
